Abbas, aged 9 is a curious kid and he likes to get his hands on to anything that is interesting.
One day, his dad was working on his laptop and had to go away to attend a phone call. Abbas started to play with it till his dad returned.
When Abbas saw his dad was returning he kept the laptop aside and started to play with something else.
His dad was shocked to see that all the work he was doing had disappeared. He got furious and had to redo the whole work again.
